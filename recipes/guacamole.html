Guacamole

Description

Gaucamole is an avocado based dip, spread or salad first devloped in Mexico. Its texture can vary from being chunky to smooth and can be had with chips or spread on a bread. It is super easy and only takes 10 minutes to prepare.

So, let's look at what you need to prepare this yummy dish

Ingredients

  • Hass Avocados - 2
  • Japapeno - 1
  • Salt - 1 tsp or to taste
  • Pepper - 1 tsp or to taste
  • Cilantro - 1/4 cup
  • Onion - 1/4 cup finely chopped
  • Garlic - 1 or 2 cloves finely chopped
  • Fresh Lime - 1 small

Steps

  1. Remove avocado flesh and place in a mixing bowl
  2. Chop Jalapenos coarsely and add to the mixing bowl along with onions and garlic
  3. Add salt, pepper and squeeze in lime juice
  4. Mix the contents either with your hands or with a masher until required texture is reached (chunky vs smooth)
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve with your favorite brand of chips, and enjoy!
