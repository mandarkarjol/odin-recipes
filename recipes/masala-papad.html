Masala Papad

Description

Masala papad is a very common appetizer in Mumbai. The papad is made of urad daal and is topped with a variety of Mumbai chaat style inredients, making it a perfect starter to any full course meal. It is super quick and extremely straight forward to prepare, and should take less than 5 mins to make

Following sections will walk you through the items you need and the method to prepare this snack/p>

Ingredients

  • Lijjat papad - 1
  • Salt - 1 tsp or to taste
  • Red Chilli Powder - 1 tsp
  • Chaat masala - 1 tsp
  • Cilantro - 1/4 cup, finely chopped
  • Onion - 1/4 cup, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1/4 cup, finely chopped
  • Green chillies - 1/2 tbsp, finely chopped
  • Sev - 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. Roast or fry papad
  2. Top papad with tomatoes, onions, green chillies, red chilli poweder, salt, chaat masala and sev
  3. Garnish with cilantro and serve!
